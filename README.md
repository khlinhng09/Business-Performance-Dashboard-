## 1. Project Title / Headline

**Business Performance Analysis Dashboard using Power BI**

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

This project focuses on building an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze overall business performance from Sep 2023 to Sep 2024.

The dashboard provides insights into revenue, customer behavior, product performance, and operational efficiency to support data-driven decision making. It helps stakeholders monitor key KPIs, evaluate sales performance, and identify business improvement opportunities.

---

## 3. Tech Stack

* **Power BI** → Dashboard development & data visualization
* **Power Query** → Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** → KPI calculations and business metrics
* **Excel / CSV Dataset** → Raw data source
* **Data Modeling** → Building relationships between Orders, Customers, Products, and Shipping tables

---

## 4. Data Source

The dataset was collected from publicly available online sources for learning and business analysis purposes.

The dataset includes:

* Orders Dataset
* Customers Dataset
* Products Dataset
* Shipping Dataset

Data period: **Sep 2023 – Sep 2024**

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

* Interactive business performance dashboard
* KPI monitoring for Revenue, Orders, Customers, and Completion Rate
* Revenue analysis by product category
* Order status analysis (Completed vs Cancelled)
* Shipping performance tracking by shipping method
* Revenue and order trend analysis over time
* Dynamic filtering by Product and Payment Method
* Monthly, quarterly, and yearly business performance analysis

---

## 6. Key Questions & Insights

### Key Questions

* How much revenue is the business generating?
* Which products contribute the most revenue?
* What is the current order completion rate?
* How do revenue and order trends change over time?
* Which shipping methods are most frequently used?
* How does business performance vary across different quarters?

### Insights

* Total revenue reached approximately **24.78M** with nearly **10K orders** and over **6.1K customers**, indicating a stable transaction volume and customer base.

* The order completion rate is **67%**, while around **33% of orders were cancelled**, suggesting that the cancellation rate remains relatively high and may impact operational efficiency.

* **Office Chair** generated the highest revenue at approximately **12M**, making it the company’s top-performing product category.

* Revenue is heavily concentrated in a few key product categories such as Office Chair and Office Desk, while products like Desk Lamp contribute very little revenue.

* Express, Overnight, and Standard shipping methods show relatively balanced order volumes (~3.3K – 3.4K orders each), indicating similar customer preferences across shipping options.

* Revenue and order volumes remained relatively stable throughout most of the year but showed a decline toward the end of the reporting period.

* Q1 and Q2 of 2024 achieved higher revenue performance compared to Q3, indicating a slight slowdown in business performance over time.

---

## 7. Overall Conclusion

The dashboard shows that the business maintains stable revenue performance and a relatively large customer base. However, revenue is heavily dependent on a few major product categories, while the cancellation rate remains high.

In addition, business performance shows signs of slight decline toward the end of the reporting period, suggesting that the company should further optimize operations, product strategy, and customer retention efforts to sustain long-term growth.

---

## 8. Recommendations

* Improve the order completion process to reduce the cancellation rate and enhance customer experience.

* Increase marketing and cross-selling activities for low-performing product categories to diversify revenue sources.

* Analyze cancellation behavior by payment method, shipping method, or customer segment to identify operational issues.

* Continuously monitor revenue trends to detect early signs of performance decline.

* Strengthen customer loyalty and retention programs to improve repeat purchase rates and long-term customer value.
